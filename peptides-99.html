Is there a membership requirement?

No. The $99 peptide offer is open to new customers with no membership required. If you decide to join the Cellebration Club afterward, every subsequent peptide session is $149 vs. $199 non-member.

Can I order multiple peptides at $99 each?

Yes. Your first order can include multiple peptides — each priced at $99. Your clinician will advise on stack compatibility during consultation.
