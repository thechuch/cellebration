Parking

Where to park.

Parking at Sunny Isles is straightforward once you know the entrance. The building has a dedicated parking garage accessible from the side street. First-time visitors: give yourself 5 extra minutes to find the ramp.

▶ Video walkthrough · Parking at Sunny Isles Pending client video — 60–90 second walkthrough

Dedicated building garage

Enter from the side street (not Collins Ave). Pull a ticket at the gate — we validate at reception, so parking is complimentary for your appointment.

Valet available

Valet is available from 10am–6pm, Mon–Sat. Hand your keys at the front circle; tell the attendant you're visiting Cellebration Suite 7.

Street parking

Metered street spots along Sunny Isles Blvd. Two-hour limit. Suitable if you're running in for a quick IV or injection; not recommended for longer sessions.

Accessibility

ADA-designated spots on the ground floor of the garage, directly adjacent to the elevator.

Arriving at the Clinic

How to find us.

  1. Step 1. From the parking garage, take the elevator up to Suite 7. Signage on the wall next to the elevator bank shows our floor.
  2. Step 2. Follow the hallway to the Cellebration door — you'll see our logo on a frosted glass panel.
  3. Step 3. Check in with reception. We keep the door unlocked during clinic hours — walk right in.
  4. Step 4. A member of our team will greet you and bring you back. First-timers start with a short intake form.

First Visit · Concierge Notes

What to expect.

  • Arrive 10 minutes early for your first visit to complete a brief health intake.
  • Bring a water bottle — hydration helps with IV and EBOO sessions.
  • Comfortable, loose-fitting clothing is ideal. Avoid tight sleeves for anything involving the arms.
  • Avoid caffeine 2 hours before PNOĒ metabolic testing.
  • If you're doing HBOT, remove watches and electronics before the session; lockers are provided.
  • Wi-Fi and refreshments are available in the waiting area. Coffee, tea, filtered water.
